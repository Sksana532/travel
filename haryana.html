6.Damdama Lake: Enjoy The Picturesque View Damdama Lake is a beautiful lake and has a calm and peaceful vibe about it. The lake was built by the British in 1947 for rainwater harvesting purpose. At present, it is home to over 190 species of the native and migratory birds. In the monsoons, water levels reach as high as fifty feet and this is when most of the migratory birds are spotted here. If you want to explore the beauty of nature, add this spot to your list of things to do in Gurgaon. It is the biggest lake in Haryana and is spread to almost three thousand acres. It is also a popular place for picnics as well as for the on-site training for corporate team-building exercises. Timings: 9:30 AM to 6 PM. Location: Damdama Lake, Haryana 122102.
